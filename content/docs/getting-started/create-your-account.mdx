## Before You Begin

- Use a work email you can access.
- Decide who will be your first admin user.
- Have your primary environment details ready (for example, production API base URL).

## Sign Up

1. Go to the HyreLog app sign-up page.
2. Enter your name, email, and password.
3. Verify your email address.
4. Complete onboarding to create your first workspace.

## First-Time Setup Checklist

- Confirm your workspace name and region.
- Invite additional team members.
- Create your first API key from the dashboard.
- Send a test event to confirm ingestion.

Continue with `/getting-started/send-your-first-event`.
